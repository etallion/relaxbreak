You're A Solo Act!

Who says you have to be social to have fun?!

Introverted people find comfort in activities and settings where other people's opinions and desires are not constantly infringing on their idea of a good time. There are endless possibilities for someone to find something enjoyable to do on their own.

Some of the attributes of someone who enjoys flying solo include: being self sufficient; becoming hyper-focused on an activity or topic; they are easy to please; they are observant; they are trustworthy; and they are committed to their goals.
